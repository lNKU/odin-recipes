Ingredients
  • 3 cups water
  • 5 tea bags
  • 1lb white cane sugar
Cooking Instructions
  1. Heat the water
  2. Put tea bags in the hot water
  3. Let steep in the water for 7 minutes
  4. Remove the tea bags and dispose
  5. Add sugar and stir
  6. Put tea in fridge to chill
